Best practices for photographing high-texture crafts?

Natural light is your best friend! Try shooting near a large window during the morning or late afternoon to get soft, diffused light that highlights the texture without harsh shadows.

Navigating International Shipping & Customs?

The Harmonized System (HS) code is key. Make sure you get the right one for your craft type. For handicrafts, it's often under Chapter 97.
